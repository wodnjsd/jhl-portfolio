A spectre floats above New York - the spectre of Karl Marx. All the powers of capital have joined to
exorcise it: the landlords, the speculators, the developers, the banks, the bureaucrats. They raise
towers, they clear streets, they make people indebted -
yet still the head of Marx returns, inflated,
multiplied, drifting across the sky.
